About JobBridge

We're building the future of professional networking, connecting talent with opportunity worldwide. Discover our story, mission, and the team behind our success.

Building the Future of Professional Networking

JobBridge was founded in 2018 with a vision to create a comprehensive platform that bridges the gap between talented professionals and forward-thinking companies. Our mission is to empower individuals to build meaningful careers and help organizations find the perfect talent.

We combine the networking power of LinkedIn, the freelance marketplace of Fiverr, and the job search capabilities of Indeed into one seamless experience designed for the modern professional.
