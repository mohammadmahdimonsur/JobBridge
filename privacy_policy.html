1. Introduction

Welcome to TalentHub's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our professional networking and freelance marketplace platform.

TalentHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. We comply with all applicable data protection laws, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other regional data protection regulations
Transparency Promise: We believe in being transparent about our data practices and giving you control over your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our platform.

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Professional background and work history
  • Skills, certifications, and portfolio items
  • Payment and billing information

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform:

  • Device information and IP address
  • Browser type and settings
  • Usage data and platform interactions
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Account Data

Information you provide when creating and maintaining your TalentHub profile.

Professional Data

Work history, skills, education, certifications, and portfolio items.

Usage Data

How you interact with our platform, features, and services.

3. How We Use Data

We use the information we collect for various business purposes to provide and improve our services.

Primary Uses

  • Providing and maintaining our platform services
  • Connecting freelancers with clients
  • Processing payments and managing billing
  • Personalizing your experience and content
  • Communicating important updates and changes

Analytics and Improvement

We use data to understand how our platform is used and to make improvements:

  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends
  • Developing new features and services
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our platform
  • Conducting research and development
Legal Basis: We only process your personal data when we have a legal basis to do so, such as your consent, contractual necessity, or legitimate interests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

We share information when you direct us to, such as when you apply for jobs or share your profile with potential clients.

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Cloud hosting services (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Communication tools (SendGrid, Twilio)
  • Analytics and monitoring services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as:

  • Responding to legal processes or government requests
  • Enforcing our terms and policies
  • Protecting the rights, property, or safety of TalentHub
Limited Sharing: We only share the minimum amount of information necessary and require our partners to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

You have important rights regarding your personal data. We provide tools and settings to help you exercise these rights.

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You can update or correct inaccurate personal data at any time.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.

Right to Restrict

You can limit how we use your personal data in specific situations.

Exercising Your Rights

You can exercise most of your rights directly through your account settings. For additional requests, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@talenthub.com.

Response Time: We respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days. Some requests may take longer if they are complex or numerous.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access and least privilege principles
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Training: Regular security awareness training for all employees

Incident Response

We have established procedures to handle potential data breaches:

  • Immediate investigation and containment
  • Notification to affected users and authorities when required
  • Post-incident analysis and improvement
Security Certifications: We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and regularly undergo independent security audits.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser or our cookie preference center:

  • Browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Opt-out tools for specific tracking technologies
  • Platform settings to manage preferences

Cookie Settings

Adjust your cookie preferences at any time through your account settings.

Opt-Out Options

Learn how to opt-out of specific tracking technologies on our platform.

Do Not Track

We respect "Do Not Track" signals and browser settings.
