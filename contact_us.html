Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure link to create a new password. The link expires in 30 minutes for security.
How long does it take to verify my account?
Account verification typically takes 1-2 business days. We verify all accounts to ensure platform security and authenticity. You'll receive an email notification once your account is verified.
Can I apply for jobs without creating an account?
No, you need to create a JobBridge account to apply for jobs. This allows employers to review your complete profile and contact you through our secure messaging system.
How does JobBridge protect my personal information?
We use enterprise-grade encryption, secure servers, and comply with global data protection regulations. Your personal information is never shared without your explicit consent.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for premium services. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.
